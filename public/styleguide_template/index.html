Typography

Headers

h1. First level headers are 30px Bold and colored #51535D.

h2. Second level headers are 21px Bold and colored #51535D.

h2. Third level headers are 18px Bold and colored #51535D.

h4. Fourth level headers are 16px Bold and colored #51535D.

h5. Fifth level headers are 14px Bold and colored #51535D.
h6. Sixth level headers are 12px and colored #768085.

Titles

Symbol titles for Symbol pages are 21 px Bold and colored with #51555D

Module Titles should be set at 16px Semibold and colored with #51555D

Article Titles should be set at 14px Bold and colored with #004B83 because they are links.

Paragraphs

Paragraphs for stories or articles are 16px big and have a line height of 1.3 for better readability. Their weight is normal/regular and are colored with #51555D. If there is a symbol present within the article, like AAPL then it should be set differently. Those should be bold and colored with #005392.

Paragraphs for summaries of articles or stories are 12px big and also have a line height of 1.3 for better readability. Their weight is also normal/regular and are also colored with #51555D. If there is a symbol present within the summary, like GOOG then it should also be set differently. It should be bold and colored with #005392.

Captions

Disclaimers and explanations that say things like "As of market close" are 10px, and they are colored #9FA0A3

Information about an article such as "2 hours ago" and "Reuters" on an Article page is 12px bold and #768085
